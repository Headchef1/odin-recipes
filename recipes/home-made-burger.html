Home made burger

Description

This is one of my favorite hamburger recipes. You can substitute bleu, gorgonzola, or feta for the Roquefort cheese.

Don't forget the American fries!

Ingredients

  • 1 Bun
  • Prepared meat patty. Approximately 125-200 grams. You can make it out of grounded meat or buy a ready to go one in a grocery.
  • 1 cut of red onion
  • Couple iceberg salad leaves
  • 1 Tomato
  • 1 Pickled cucumber
  • 1-2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • BBQ souse

Steps

  1. Before start baking, prepare all ingredients. Slice tomatoes, pickles, cheese, onion. Wash salad leaves, and can chop them to fit in to a bun
  2. Heat up a pan, if you cook at home, or prepare a grill
  3. Bake buns on a pan or grill for a minute or less to make it a bit crispy. Try not to overcook it or burn them. When buns are ready, turn off the heat but don't hide the pan.
  4. Place all ingredients starting from the bottom bun. Salad, tomato, pickles, onion
  5. On the top bun spill some souse
  6. Baking patty:
    1. Bake it on each side for 1 min flipping it over
    2. You can dress it up with some pepper and oregano
    3. After each side backed for 1 min, continue to bake each side for 2 more minutes
    4. For las several seconds put cheese slices on patty for it to start melting
  7. Take patty of the pan ot grill and place it on prepared bun

Sum up

And that should be it. If you have fries for garnish you can add them for full meal.

Such easy recipe will make you a king of a grill this evening.
